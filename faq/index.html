These are some of the most asked questions from our viewers. Answer to each question has been thoughtfully prepared by Dr. Ashish himself.
1. In meditation, what is more important, the stillness of mind or feeling the energy in the body?

Most important thing in meditation is neither stillness of mind nor feeling the energy of the body. There is something more important than both these and that is clarity/understanding. Your own understanding about the movement of your thoughts and why they are moving the way they are.

Your psychic energy is most important to go into deeper states if meditation and the movement of thoughts is what drains away this  energy.

2. What are specific meditation techniques for enhancing focus and memory?

Yoga is a spiritual science that has 8 steps. Yama, Niyama,Asana, Pranayama, Pratyahara, Dharana, Dhyan & Samadhi.

Focus of mind can only be increased when we master all these steps. Improvement of memory and concentration is a by product of the same.
